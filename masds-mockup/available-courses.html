Elective rules

Electives help students customize the degree while preserving the 44-unit requirement.

400-level statistics electivesElective courses can emphasize statistical modeling, programming, applied analytics, and advanced methods.
Approved 200-level coursesMASDS students may request selected 200-level courses numbered between 203 and 283, subject to restrictions and space.
Statistics 496 InternshipAn approved work project or internship may count for up to 4 units of elective credit.
Statistics 497 Individual StudiesApproved individual study may count for up to 4 units toward the elective requirement.
Course waiversUp to three core courses may be waived if equivalent graduate-level coursework has already been completed. Waived core courses are replaced with electives.
Enrollment notesStudents outside MASDS may not take MASDS 400-level courses. Some 200-level requests may be limited by course capacity.

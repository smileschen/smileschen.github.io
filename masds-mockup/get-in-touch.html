What to ask before applying

Prospective students often benefit from asking about academic preparation, work-life pacing, course load, and thesis opportunities.

Academic fitAsk whether your math, statistics, programming, and professional background are aligned with the program.
Schedule fitAsk how evening classes and one- or two-course quarters can work with your professional responsibilities.
Career fitAsk about networking events, industry connections, thesis projects, and career outcomes.

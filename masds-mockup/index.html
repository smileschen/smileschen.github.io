First, decide whether MASDS fits your life and goals.

MASDS is designed for students who want rigorous graduate training in statistics and data science while continuing to grow professionally.

1 You want rigorous statistical foundations. Build depth in statistical judgment, modeling, uncertainty, computing, and applied data science.
2 You need a working-professional format. Attend evening classes in person while continuing to work, build experience, and apply what you learn.
3 You want a career-oriented UCLA credential. Connect coursework with thesis projects, industry relationships, alumni outcomes, and a UCLA graduate credential.

The in-person format is a defining advantage.

The in-person experience gives students regular access to faculty, classmates, campus resources, and professional networking opportunities in Los Angeles.

Learning environment Face-to-face learning creates real mastery. Students meet faculty and classmates in person, ask questions live, present work, and complete assessments in a structured academic setting that supports integrity and genuine skill-building.
UCLA signal A prestigious degree with campus presence. Earn a graduate degree from UCLA's Department of Statistics and Data Science, part of one of the world's leading public research universities.
Professional network Evening classes keep careers moving. The schedule lets working professionals continue gaining experience while building a UCLA peer network through classes, industry events, thesis projects, and partner connections.

Move through a 44-unit curriculum at a pace that fits your life.

Students complete core courses, electives, and a written thesis. Take two classes per quarter to finish in about two years, or take one class in busier quarters and continue at a more flexible pace.
